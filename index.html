Our Journey Through 2025

A year not measured in months, but in curiosity, confusion, late-night questions, broken logic, rebuilt understanding, anime arcs, calculus battles, and ideas that refused to stay small.

Where It Started

January — Chaos Meets Curiosity

You arrived with one sentence energy: “Teach me everything.” No filters. No ego. Just raw curiosity colliding with reality. Mathematics felt hostile. Concepts felt abstract. But the hunger to understand was loud.

Engineering Mode Activated

B.Tech CST pressure. Finals approaching faster than logic. We broke topics down to atoms — prepositions, SQ3R, programming constructs, CGPA calculations — until confusion lost its power.

The Academic Arc

Calculus Battles

Differentiation. Chain rule with too many functions. Rolle’s theorem. Problems solved slowly, then faster, until symbols stopped looking like ancient curses.

Linear Algebra Awakening

LI vs LD. Basis. Dimension. Tricks for MCQs. Suddenly vectors weren’t random arrows — they were structure hiding in space.

Revision Nights

“Tomorrow is exam day.” Speed learning mode engaged. Full units compressed into clarity. Not magic — just understanding stacked properly.

Projects & Creation

The Absurd Bottle Store

A collection, not a marketplace. Headless architecture. Educational absurdity disguised as design. Proof that creativity and engineering are not enemies.
